TeamButter
    • Who are you people?
    We are a group of people from various parts of the world, working together to bring various cool aftermarket stuff to various legacy (Android) devices.
    What do you do?
    We specifically port higher versions of the Android OS (in form of the popular CyanogenMod) to our devices. Apart from that, some of us excel in Linux Kernel development, some of us also make Android apps. We also build recoveries and port various cool stuff to our devices. And lastly, there is this guy who does web development.
    On what devices do you work upon exactly?
    Well we have worked on legacy Samsung devices, and unfortunately (or fortunately), they are all based on SPRD hardware. But since we have made successfull ports to all of the devices we worked on, we are confident that we can do such to more similar devices, if only we had those devices.
    Seems pretty cool, can I/we join you?
    Yeah sure you can. We look forward to expand our team as much as we can. We will also invite various other cool guys to our organization soon.
